Chocolate Muffins

Cook Time 25 Minutes
Serving Size 1 Muffin
Yield a dozen
Prep Time 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup semiswee chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line the muffin pan with cupcake liners
  3. Combine flour, sugar, chocolate chips, cocoa powder, and baking soda in a large bowl.
  4. Whisk yogurt, milk, oil, egg, and vanilla in a separate bowl until smooth.
  5. Pour yogurt mixture into the chocolate mixture and stir until the batter is blended.
  6. Fill prepared muffin cups 3/4 full and sprinkle with the remaining 1/4 cup chocolate chips.
  7. Bake in oven for 25 minute
  8. Let Cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
