Blueberry Muffin

Cook Time 20 Minutes
Serving Size 1 Muffin
Yield a dozen
Prep Time 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cups of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/3 cup of avocado/sunflower oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 8 ounces of frozen bluebeerries

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Line the muffin pan with cupcake liners
  3. Whisk the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl
  4. In a seperate bowl add oil, milk, egg, and vanilla extract, whisk to combine
  5. Divide the batter between muffin cups, filling each 3/4 of the way full
  6. Bake muffins 15 to 20 minutes or until top no longer looks wet
  7. Transfer muffins to a cooling rack and let them cool for 10 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
