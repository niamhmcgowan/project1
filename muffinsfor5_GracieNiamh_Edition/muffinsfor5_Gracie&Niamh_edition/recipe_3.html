Corn Muffin

Cook Time 11-13 minutes
Serving Size 1 Muffin
Yield a dozen
Prep Time 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of cornmeal
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of sea salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 1 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 3 large eggs

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Line the muffin pan with cupcake liners
  3. Whisk the flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl
  4. In a seperate bowl add the milk, butter, oil, and eggs, whisk to combine
  5. slowly pour the wet ingridients into the flour mixture and whisk
  6. Fill muffin cups 2/3 of the way full and place in oven
  7. Bake for 12 minutes
  8. Let muffins sit on cooling rack for 10 minutes
