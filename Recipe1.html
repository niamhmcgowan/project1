Chocolate muffin

cook time Serving size yield prep time
25 minutes 12 dozen 20 minutes

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup white sugar
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips, divided
½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 cup plain yogurt
½ cup milk
½ cup vegetable oil
1 large egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line 12 muffin cups with paper muffin liners.
  3. Combine flour, sugar, 3/4 cup chocolate chips, cocoa powder, and baking soda in a large bowl.
  4. Whisk yogurt, milk, oil, egg, and vanilla in a separate bowl until smooth.
  5. Pour yogurt mixture into the chocolate mixture and stir until the batter is just blended.

    6. Dough all mixed together in a bowl with a spoon

  6. Fill prepared muffin cups 3/4 full and sprinkle with the remaining 1/4 cup chocolate chips.

    7. Close up of muffin tin with liners and batter in the cups

  7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, about 20 minutes.
  8. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
  9. Now you are ready to enjoy your delicous muffins with the family!
