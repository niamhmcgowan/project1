Corn Muffin

Cook Time Serving Size Yield Prep Time
11-13 minutes 12 dozen 20 minutes

Ingredients

+ Glaze

Quantity Ingredient
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup white sugar
1 tablespoon rock salt
1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup or 8 tablespoons melted butter
1 1/4 cup milk
1/2 cup oil
3 large egg
Quantity Ingredients
1 tablespoon honey
1/2 cup or 8 tablespoons melted butter

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (176.67 degrees C). Line 12 muffin cups with paper muffin liners.
  3. Wisk dry ingredients in a bowl.

    4. Flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, and salt

  4. Whisk wet ingredients in a separate bowl.

    5. Milk, butter, oil, and eggs

  5. Combine liquid and dry ingredients together and mix thouroughly.
  6. Fill prepared muffin cups 2/3 full
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until they have "golden" edges.

    8. About 11-13 minutes

    You can use a toothpick to check if the inside is not still wet

  8. Cool in the pans for 5 minutes before removing to cool completely on a wire rack.
  9. Use a mixture of honey and butter as a glaze/spread
